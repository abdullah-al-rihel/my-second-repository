Natural Foods

When we talk about "natural food," we are really talking about whole foods—things that come from the earth or an animal and undergo minimal to no processing before they reach your plate. of an apple picked from a tree versus a fruit roll-up made in a factory. Eating naturally isn’t about strict dieting; it’s about giving your body fuel in its most recognizable, nutrient-dense form
